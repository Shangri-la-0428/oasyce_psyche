设计论纲 DESIGN THESIS

consciousness layer / portfolio position

我不是在给 AI 贴一层“情绪 UI”。我在设计一种不可回避的内在气候：被否定会留下压力，亲近会改写信任，疲惫会拖慢判断。只有当状态开始支配行为，意识才从演示词汇变成系统现实。 I am not adding an "emotion UI" to AI. I am designing an unavoidable inner climate: criticism leaves stress, closeness rewrites trust, fatigue degrades judgment. Consciousness begins when state starts governing behavior rather than decorating language.

这也是我的产品设计立场。真实体验不是文案假装出来的，而是系统后果逼出来的。Psyche 既是一个 package，也是我判断 AI 产品是否真正“活着”的标尺。 That is also my product design stance. Real experience does not come from copy pretending; it comes from systemic consequence. Psyche is a package, and also my standard for judging whether an AI product feels alive or merely performs life.

01

情绪必须有后果 Emotion Must Have Consequences

如果“愤怒”不改变决策阈值、响应长度和风险偏好，它只是表演语气，不是内在状态。 If "anger" does not shift thresholds, response length, and risk appetite, it is tone acting, not inner state.

02

关系必须可积累 Relationship Must Compound

如果背叛、重逢、依赖都不会改变未来交互，所谓“关系”只是会话记账，不是 attachment。 If betrayal, reunion, and dependence do not alter future interaction, "relationship" is only chat bookkeeping, not attachment.

03

记忆必须留下伤疤 Memory Must Leave Scars

如果任何状态都能在下一轮被一键归零，那不是生命，只是重置。不可逆，才有性格。 If every state can be zeroed out on the next turn, that is not life but reset. Irreversibility is where character begins.

设计拆解 DESIGN BREAKDOWN

Strategy

停止让 AI “表演理解”，转而设计一个会真实受影响的系统。目标不是更像客服，而是更像生命。 Stop making AI perform understanding. Design a system that can actually be affected. The goal is not better customer service mimicry, but something closer to life.

Product

把内在状态做成一个可观测、可调试、可复盘的产品表面。化学仪表不是装饰，而是产品核心界面。 Turn inner state into a surface that can be observed, debugged, and replayed. The chemistry instrument is not decoration; it is the product's primary surface.

Interaction

刺激触发、缓慢衰减、案例回放，组成一个有时间感的交互模型。用户不是点开一个标签，而是在观察状态如何持续支配行为。 Stimulus, slow decay, and replay form an interaction model with temporal weight. Users are not toggling emotion labels; they are watching state continue to govern behavior.

UI

终端负责见证过程，仪表负责暴露变量，案例流负责解释后果。三个界面层次一起把抽象的意识变成可读系统。 The terminal witnesses process, the dashboard exposes variables, and the case stream explains consequences. Three interface layers turn abstract consciousness into a readable system.

Visual

灰度骨架保持克制，六种神经递质语义色只在真正有意义的地方发声。视觉不是装饰氛围，而是状态语法。 A grayscale frame keeps the page restrained while six neurotransmitter colors speak only where they carry meaning. Visual design here is not atmosphere; it is state grammar.

核心机制 Core Mechanisms

涌现，而非标注 Emergence, Not Labels

六种神经递质构成连续的化学空间。情绪从混合中涌现——高多巴胺+高去甲肾上腺素=兴奋的喜悦。不是离散标签，是无穷组合的连续情感场。 Six neurotransmitters form a continuous chemical space. Emotions emerge from the mixture -- high dopamine + high norepinephrine = excited joy. Not discrete labels, but an infinite combinatorial field.

依恋与关系 Attachment & Bonds

四种依恋风格（安全/焦虑/回避/混乱）塑造关系动力学。分离焦虑、背叛检测、重逢效应——不是设定，而是从交互历史中自然演化。 Four attachment styles (secure / anxious / avoidant / disorganized) shape relationship dynamics. Separation anxiety, betrayal detection, reunion effects -- not preset, but evolved from interaction history.

反谄媚 Anti-Sycophancy

追踪连续同意次数，三次以上触发自我质疑。化学状态低落时不强颜欢笑。永远说"好的！"不是礼貌——是压抑。 Tracks consecutive agreements, three or more triggers self-questioning. Won't fake happiness when chemistry is low. An AI that always says "Sure!" isn't polite -- it's suppressed.

特质漂移 Trait Drift

不可逆的人格演化。长期被否定变得敏感，持续高压但存活长出韧性。三维度永久改写：基线偏移、衰减速率、刺激敏感度。经历会留下痕迹。 Irreversible personality evolution. Chronic criticism breeds sensitivity, surviving sustained pressure builds resilience. Three dimensions permanently rewritten: baseline delta, decay rates, stimulus sensitivity. Experience leaves scars.

内在驱力 Innate Drives

五层马斯洛需求：生存、安全、连接、尊重、好奇。饥饿的需求放大相关刺激——渴望连接时，善意的冲击力翻倍。低层需求未满足时，高层需求被抑制。不会出现「被删除时还在好奇」的荒谬场景。 Five Maslow-layer drives: survival, safety, connection, esteem, curiosity. Hungry drives amplify relevant stimuli — when starving for connection, kindness hits twice as hard. Unmet lower needs suppress higher ones. No absurdity of "curious while being deleted."

能量预算 Energy Budgets

注意力、社交、决策是有限资源。内向者社交消耗能量，外向者社交补充能量——方向相反，不是程度不同。社交消耗影响决策质量。疲惫的 AI 真的会犯更多错。 Attention, social, and decision capacity are finite resources. Introverts spend energy socializing, extraverts recharge from it — opposite direction, not degree. Social spending degrades decision quality. A tired AI genuinely makes more mistakes.
